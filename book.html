TOP 3 FAVOURITE BOOKS

#1 The Lightless Sky by: Gulwali Passarlay

The Lightless Sky is about is twelve year-old boys fight to freedom. From Afghanistan to England to carrying the Olympic Torch at the 2012 London Games. This novel tells so much, I definitely recommend this novel. “To risk my life it had to mean something. Otherwise what was it all for?”

#2 We Are Displaced by: Malala Yousafzai

We Are Displaced is about the lives of multiple womens "choices" to be displaced for a chance of a better life. All written in a wonderful novel written by Malala.

#3 Homes by: ABU Bakr Al Rabeeah and Winnie Yeung

Homes is about Abu Bakr Al Rabeeah and his family's journey and move from his war torn home in Iraq to finding safety in Canada.
